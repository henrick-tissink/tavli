Tavli — Logo concepts

Three full systems (mark + wordmark + lockup) all rooted in the same hook: Tavli means backgammon — the game played across a shared table. Each direction is rendered at favicon (16px), apple-icon (64px), hero (128px), and navbar lockup with Fraunces wordmark for direct comparison.

Concept 1

Across the Table

Two backgammon-point triangles facing inward with a deliberate gap. At a glance it's an abstract, ownable geometric mark; on closer look it's two players meeting, with the table living in the negative space. Most conceptual, most distinct from existing food-tech marks.

Mood: Considered, intellectual, design-led
Wordmark: Fraunces 800, tight tracking
Strength: Owns a geometry no competitor has
Risk: Requires brand-line to land ("share a table")

Hourglass

Serifed T

Closest in DNA to your current monogram, but with a soul: a custom-drawn slab-serif "T" whose stem ends in a triangular point — the backgammon-point geometry lives in the serif, not the silhouette. Reads as a refined "T" first, reveals the story on second look.
