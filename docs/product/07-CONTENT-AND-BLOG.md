# Content Strategy & Blog — ialoc.ro

## Blog: "La cafea" (At Coffee)

**URL:** `https://ialoc.ro/lacafea/`
**Tagline:** "Povești proaspete despre restaurante, baruri și cafenele" (Fresh stories about restaurants, bars, and cafes)
**Platform:** WordPress (separate from main site)

### Content Types

1. **Top Lists / Listicles**
   - "8 restaurante din București cu muzică live"
   - "8 restaurante child friendly din București"
   - Numbered, city-specific, themed around lifestyle/occasion

2. **Business Testimonials / Case Studies**
   - "Cum a reușit Hard Rock Cafe să digitalizeze rezervările" (How Hard Rock Cafe digitized reservations)
   - "Despre beneficiile ialoc business – interviu cu Viktorio Salic, co-fondator leBAB"
   - Dual purpose: content marketing for consumers + social proof for B2B sales

3. **Restaurant Recommendations**
   - Curated guides by theme, occasion, or cuisine
   - Integrated with ialoc's collection system

### Blog Integration on Main Site
- Blog articles surfaced on **city landing pages** (4 recent articles in a horizontal carousel)
- Each article card has: featured image, title, clickable link
- "Citește mai multe pe blog" (Read more on blog) CTA link

## Content Strategy Observations

- Content is primarily **Romanian language only**
- Blog is **low-frequency** — articles appear infrequent
- Focus on **Bucharest** — most content targets the capital
- **SEO-driven** top lists targeting specific search queries
- B2B testimonials serve dual purpose (content + sales enablement)
- Newsletter and social media serve as **retention channels**
- Podcast appears to be a minor/dormant channel
