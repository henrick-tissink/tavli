# B2B / Restaurant Owner Features — ialoc.ro

## Overview

ialoc offers a comprehensive B2B platform at `restaurant.ialoc.ro` branded as **ialoc Business**. The about page describes three product verticals:

## 1. ialoc Reservation & Discovery

- **Premium online presence** — restaurant profile with photos, description, menu, hours
- **Direct reservations without commission** — restaurants receive bookings for free (or low cost)
- **Embeddable reservation widget** — can be integrated into the restaurant's own website
- **In-app and on-site promotion** — featured in ialoc's app and website
- **Real-time availability management** — restaurants control their table availability
- **New customer acquisition** — digital channel for attracting diners

## 2. ialoc Business (Reservation Management)

- **Table management system** — maximize table occupancy
- **Reservation management dashboard** — manage incoming bookings
- **Customer experience tools** — track and respond to reviews
- **Review response system** — restaurants can publicly reply to reviews (seen in action on restaurant detail pages — responses include manager name and title)

## 3. ialoc QR Ordering & Payments

- **QR code-based ordering** — customers scan a code at the table
- **Digital menu** — no app download required
- **Order directly from phone** — select dishes, customize
- **Online payment at table** — pay without waiting for the bill
- **Tipping** — digital tipping included
- **POS integration** — orders transmitted directly to restaurant's POS system
- **Error reduction** — no manual order transcription
- **Uninterrupted service flow** — no waiter needed for ordering/payment

## B2B Promotion on Consumer Site

- Footer CTA on every page: "Ai un restaurant, bar sau cafenea? Află mai multe despre soluțiile ialoc Business"
- Links to `restaurant.ialoc.ro`
- Blog content includes testimonials from restaurant owners (e.g., Hard Rock Cafe digitalization story, leBAB co-founder interview)

## Revenue Model (Inferred)

- Freemium/subscription model for restaurants
- Possibly commission-free reservations (differentiator vs. TheFork)
- QR ordering likely a paid add-on
- Premium placement/recommendations may be paid
- Discount promotions suggest marketing tools for restaurants
