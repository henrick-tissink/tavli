# Tavli — Vision & Differentiation

## The Name

**Tavli** = **masă** (Romanian for "table") + **ro** (Romania)

- Rolls off the tongue, easy to pronounce internationally
- Evokes the core experience: finding your table
- Sounds warm, inviting, slightly Italian (resonates with dining culture)
- Domain potential: tavli.ro, tavli.app
- Works as a verb: "Let's Tavli tonight" / "Tavli it"

### Alternative Names Considered
| Name | Meaning | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| **Tavli** | masă + ro | Winner. Warm, memorable, international |
| Gustaro | gust (taste) + ro | Good but sounds like a snack brand |
| Locanda | Italian for "inn" | Elegant but too Italian-sounding for a Romanian brand |
| Terasa | Romanian for "terrace" | Limits the brand to outdoor dining |
| Masa.ro | Direct "table" | Too literal, hard to brand |

## What Makes Tavli Different from ialoc

### 1. Visual-First Discovery
- **Map-based browsing** — interactive map as a primary discovery mode (like Airbnb)
- **Photo-rich listing cards** — large hero images, not tiny thumbnails
- **Instagram-quality venue profiles** — full-screen photo galleries, video support
- **Neighborhood guides** — editorial content about each zone, not just filter labels

### 2. Smart & Personal
- **User accounts with taste profiles** — track preferences, favorites, reservation history
- **AI-powered recommendations** — "Based on places you've loved" / "Trending near you"
- **Time-aware surfacing** — "Open now", "Available tonight", "Last-minute tables"
- **Smart search** — autocomplete, trending, "Find me a romantic Italian place near Herăstrău for 4 people tonight"

### 3. Social & Community
- **Photo reviews** — let diners share their food photos
- **Helpful/not helpful** on reviews — surface the most useful feedback
- **Social sharing** — shareable restaurant cards, "Share this spot" flows
- **Lists & collections** — users can create and share their own curated lists
- **Follow restaurants** — get notified about new menus, events, or offers

### 4. Premium Design Language
- **Warm color palette** — amber, cream, deep green, terracotta (evokes dining warmth)
- **Typography** — elegant serif for headings, clean sans-serif for body
- **Micro-interactions** — smooth transitions, delightful hover states, pull-to-refresh on mobile
- **Dark mode** — beautiful dark theme for evening browsing
- **Mobile-first** — designed for phones first, scaled up for desktop

### 5. Enhanced Reservations
- **Real-time table visualization** — see available tables on a floor plan
- **Special occasion tools** — birthday, anniversary, business dinner presets
- **Group booking** — coordinate with friends, split the table
- **Waitlist** — "Notify me when a table opens"
- **Modification & cancellation** — easy in-app changes

### 6. For Restaurants (B2B)
- **Analytics dashboard** — understand your diners, peak times, review sentiment
- **Marketing tools** — targeted promotions, loyalty programs, seasonal campaigns
- **Integrated payments** — end-to-end from reservation to payment to review
- **Multi-location management** — for restaurant groups

## Technical Vision

- **Next.js** — SSR for SEO + SPA-like interactivity
- **React Native** or **Flutter** — true native mobile apps
- **Mapbox** or **Google Maps Platform** — first-class map integration
- **Algolia** or **Typesense** — instant search with faceting
- **PostgreSQL** + **PostGIS** — spatial queries for nearby/distance
- **Cloudflare** — edge caching, image optimization
- **Stripe** or local processor — payments infrastructure

## Competitive Landscape in Romania

| Platform | Positioning | Gap Tavli Fills |
|----------|------------|------------------|
| ialoc.ro | Market leader, functional but dated | Beautiful UX, modern features, personalization |
| Restograf.ro | Reviews-focused (like TripAdvisor) | Integrated reservation + discovery |
| TheFork (by TripAdvisor) | International player | Local expertise, Romanian-first, warmer brand |
| Google Maps | Default discovery | Dedicated dining experience, reservations, community |

## Summary

Tavli isn't just a prettier ialoc.ro — it's a reimagination of how Romanians discover, book, and experience dining out. Where ialoc is a utility (search > filter > book), Tavli is an experience (discover > fall in love > share > return).
